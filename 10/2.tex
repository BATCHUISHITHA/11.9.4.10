Q: Find the sum to $n$ terms of the series whose $n^{th}$ term is given by $(2n-1)^2$ ?

\solution

Sum of $n$ terms of AP is given by
\begin{align}
 y(n)&=\sum_{k=0}^{n} x(k)\\
    &=x(n)*u(n)  
\end{align}
\begin{align}
x(n)&= (2n+1)^2 u(n) \\
X(z)&= \sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty} x(n)z^{-n} \\
&=\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}(2n+1)^2 z^{-n} \\
&=\frac{4z^{-1}(z^{-1}+1)}{(1-z^{-1})^3} + \frac{1}{(1-z^{-1})} + \frac{4z^{-1}}{(1-z^{-1})^2}
\end{align}
\begin{align}
    Y(z) &= X(z)U(z) \\
    &= (\frac{4z^{-1}(z^{-1}+1)}{(1-z^{-1})^3} + \frac{1}{(1-z^{-1})} + \frac{4z^{-1}}{(1-z^{-1})^2})(\frac{1}{1-z^{-1}})\quad |z|>1\\
    &= \frac{4z^{-1}(z^{-1}+1)}{(1-z^{-1})^4} + \frac{1}{(1-z^{-1})^2} + \frac{4z^{-1}}{(1-z^{-1})^3}\quad  |z|>1 \\
    &=  \frac{1+9z^{-1}}{(1-z^{-1})} + \frac{25z^{-2}}{(1-z^{-1})^2} + \frac{24z^{-3}}{(1-z^{-1})^3} + \frac{8z^{-4}}{(1-z^{-1})^4} \quad |z|>1
\end{align}
On solving, we get
\begin{align}
    y\brak{n} &=(\frac{4n^{3}+12n^{2}+11n+3}{3} )u(n)
\end{align}

Therefore, sum of $n$ terms of the series whose $n^{th}$ term is given by $(2n+1)^2$ is $\frac{n(4n^2-1)}{3} $  .
